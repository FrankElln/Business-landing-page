The best pizza – the best service

At Paulie's Pizzeria, we serve the best pizza you'll find anywhere in the country. Whether you visit one of our many locations or order from home, our staff are dedicated to providing you excellent service!

24 hours/7 days

We are open 24 hours, 7 days a week. Paulie's Pizzeria pizzas are the perfect meal for any occasion, no matter what time it is!

30 minutes or free

Sudden dinner plans? No problem! Whether you join us at one of our pizzeria or order from home, we will have your pizza ready in 30 minutes, or it's free!

100% organic

All our ingredients are fresh, organic, and local. Our vegetables, dairy, and bread are harvested without added hormones or pesticides. Good for your health and your home!

Any toppings

Pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, pineapple, you name it! We have over 100 toppings and will make a pizza with any combo of your choosing. It's up to you!

How to order – Simple as 1, 2, 3

1

Download our app on your electronic device and sign up today.

2

Select your pizza size and what toppings you would like on it. Or you can call your local Paulie's Pizzeria!

3

In less than 30 minutes, you will have your pizza delivered to you or ready for pickup. Enjoy!
